Vinoth Chakkaravarthy Head of the Department Welcome to the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, where we're dedicated to cultivate critical thinking and technical expertise. We aspire to shape not just intelligent professionals but individuals with strong character, integrity, and a commitment to excellence. Your journey here promises a holistic and enriching educational experience. Origin and Advancement The Department of Computer Science and Engineering, established in 2007 with an initial intake of 60 students and a faculty team of four, has blossomed into a distinguished centre of excellence in computer science education and research. Currently, the department maintains an impressive enrolment of 664 students, under the guidance of a strong and dedicated teaching and non-teaching faculty comprising 24 experts. Program Offerings and Facilities Our department is committed to nurturing excellence in computer science and engineering through a comprehensive range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. Recognized as an approved Research Center by Anna University, Chennai. We prioritize cutting-edge research in information and communication engineering and offer honours degrees across diverse domains. With our state-of-the-art computer labs and centralized monitoring facilities, we strive to create a dynamic and supportive learning environment for our diverse student community. Comprehensive Self-Evaluation Approach At our Computer Science Department, we use a thorough self-evaluation process to ensure we offer the best education possible. Here are some key aspects: 1. Regular Assessment and Improvement We continuously evaluate and enhance our programs to stay aligned with the rapidly evolving tech landscape. This ongoing process ensures our courses remain current and effective, enabling us to offer high-quality education that meets the dynamic needs of today's technology-driven world. 2. Faculty Expertise and Curriculum Our department thrives on the expertise and dedication of our faculty members, who are well-qualified and experienced, ensuring we meet the teaching and research demands of our students. Our curriculum spans the entire spectrum of computer science and engineering, encompassing hardware and system programming, application development, networking and distributed computing, data engineering and data analytics,software engineeringand theoretical computer science. 3. Specialized Elective Courses In addition to our comprehensive curriculum, the department is committed to providing students with specialized domain-specific electives that cater to their individual interests and career aspirations. These elective courses allow our students to delve deeper into specific areas of technology and innovation, further enhancing their skill sets and knowledge. Our selection of domain-specific electives includes data science, full-stack development,data centre technologies, cyber security and data privacy, creative media, progressive technologies and cognitive computing. 4. Practical Learning At our institution, we prioritize practical learning. Our well-equipped labs and resources empower students to apply classroom knowledge in real-world projects. These hands-on experiences prepare students for the workforce by bridging the gap between theory and practice, fostering valuable skills, and enhancing their readiness for professional challenges. 5. On-Campus Placements We take pride in our on-campus placement program, where our students have the opportunity to interview with and secure positions in renowned companies. Our placement record includes top companies, and many of our graduates have been placed with high packages, some as high as 25 LPA (Lakhs Per Annum). We're dedicated to provide a top-notch education in computer science and ensuring our students have the best opportunities for both academic success and successful careers, including high-paying placements with prestigious companies. 6. Research and Industry Collaborations We've established strategic alliances via Memoranda of Understanding (MoUs) with over 15 industries, which enable us to place a strong emphasis on student internships, workshops, seminars, and faculty training programs.Our commitment to research is underscored by the acquisition of substantial funds totalling Rs. 111.96 Lakhs from esteemed institutions such as DST, DRDO, MNRE, ISRO, and IEI.
